About Random Orbital Sandpaper

Who We Are

Random Orbital Sandpaper is a focused resource for anyone who uses a random orbital sander. We cover 5-inch hook-and-loop sanding discs — the most common format for orbital sanders from brands like DeWalt, Makita, Bosch, Festool, and others.

Our goal is simple: help you pick the right sandpaper for your project without wading through hundreds of Amazon listings. We research products based on manufacturer specifications, user feedback, and general industry knowledge to provide straightforward recommendations.

How We Make Recommendations

Our recommendations are based on publicly available product specifications, manufacturer data, and widely reported user experiences. We aggregate information from multiple sources to provide informed guidance.

We consider factors like grain type, dust extraction design, available grit ranges, price per disc, and general reputation within the woodworking and auto body communities.
